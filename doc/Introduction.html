    ElixirBridge v0.0.1 Introduction

    Welcome to the Airport

    You made it! Let’s go through some information before you get on the plane.

    On each destination you’ll receive information of your next trip.

    Here are suggestions for your safe and successful travels.

    • Remember that you are a great student and you are here to create solutions.
    • We encourage you to ask if you don’t understand or know something.

    If you get lost, don’t hesitate to ask the teacher or your teammates. And remember you can contact a 24/7 community that is spread around the world. They’re great people and love to help. The channels you can use:

    1. Slack. Just access and choose your username.
    2. Mailing List. Subscribe in order to send messages to the community.
    3. IRC. Good old IRC is the precursor of Slack. You can chat right away here.

    Run!

    Go to Gate G99 and catch your flight! See you there.
